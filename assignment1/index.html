About Me

On Dec 8, 2013 President Barack Obama asks every American to give it a shot to learn to computer science, kicking off the Hour of Code campaign for Computer Science Education Week 2013. http://code.org. I thought it would be cool to check it out and from that day I have been digging for more information. I started a few free intro-to-code online lessons on websites like Codecademy or Khan Academy and then migrated to Treehouse and Code School.

As a fullstack developer I work with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, Responsive Design, Node.js, React.js, Heroku, User Authentication, MongoDB, MySQL, PHP, Ruby and Swift. Click on the links for more info.
